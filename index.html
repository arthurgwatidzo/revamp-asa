Our Founder & Managing Director

Arthur Gwatidzo

Founder & Managing Director

Arthur Gwatidzo has over 14 years working experience in the Information Technology sector. Arthur spent 9 years fulfilling full stack software development duties in different speciality industry fields such as the banking, agricultural, training and health sectors in both Zimbabwe and South Africa. Arthur also previously assumed responsibilities for network administration, database administration and training duties. Arthur has worked for leading international companies including Investec Bank, Accenture, Discovery, First National Bank (FNB), Pearson Education Group South Africa, Interfin Bank Zimbabwe amongst other companies. Arthur completed a Bachelor of Science Honors degree in Computer Science at the Midlands State University in 2006.

Managing Director's Message

Companies are fast seizing on Cloud computing as the key enabler to complete their digital transformation, and the COVID pandemic has further accelerated endorsement by most top C-Suite personnel in MANCO & EXCO board meetings. At ASA, we assist our clients to think strategically about desired business outcomes, investment priorities, deployment models, service providers, talent sourcing and cloud policies. At Africa Software Architects we believe that when companies adopt cloud strategies as part of their digital transformation journey, these companies will significantly reduce IT infrastructure costs, increase time-to-market for their products and services to the international market. ASA is a AWS digital consulting partner and an Oracle Partner Network member and these business synergies will help us to quickly extend dependable & efficient Cloud solutions and Services to all our clients.
